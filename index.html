About Me

I am currently a fourth-year Audio Engineering Major at Michigan Tech. I am pursuing this major to be able to express myself and my passions for technology, music, and design in the entertainment industry through sound. I am also the current president for the Audio Engineering Society here at Michigan Tech, where I work on designing loudspeakers and systems for events and other projects, one of my biggest passions since attending Tech. I also enjoy working in broadcasting and production, another passion of mine that I pursue while working for our esports organization as a broadcast production manager. I have been a part of the program since 2019 and am one of the first members hired on the broadcasting team. Outside of work, my hobbies include music, guitar, video games, streaming, and hockey. My hobbies have brought me many friendships and connections that I continue to cherish in my life.
